About Us

TechRise DTI is a non-profit organization committed to driving digital transformation in Africa through grassroots education, youth empowerment, and tech-enabled community development.

groups We work directly with local communities to understand their unique needs and co-create sustainable solutions.

computer Equipping young Africans with digital skills and entrepreneurial mindsets to lead the continent's transformation.

construction Building long-term capacity and infrastructure that communities can own and maintain independently.
